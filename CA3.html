Here are some smart and easy tips

  • limit the amount of information you post about yourself : So that strangers will not know too much.
  • Use strong passwords : It will very hard for someone else to guess.
  • Check privacy policies of the app/website : It can constantly spam your mail if you do not check.
  • Be cautios of third-party users : Avoid all those seems suspicious.
  • Evaluate your settings : There could be a possibility that you persona information is not secured, so check your settings.
  • Limit access privileges : Only allow authorized staff access your social media profiles or your websites that you visit.
  • Be awawre of free wi-fi : As much it is free, people can access it easily as it has unecrypted networks.
  • Enable two-factor authentication : This will increase your security by requiring a second step of verification.
  • Be cautios and selective when accepting new requests : Cross refer that specific account to see if it really is real or not.
  • close your old accounts that are inactive : Don't take the risk of leaving personal data in it as you yourself could forget about it and make it easier for the hacker.
